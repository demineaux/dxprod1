About Fields Appliance Service

Fields Appliance Service, Inc. was established in June of 1970 by Troy and Dot Fields.
Today our General Manager is Brian Fields. We have been family owned and operated in the Orlando Market for over 48 years.

We currently have over 55 technicians located throughout Central Florida. We realize that the success of our company relies on the satisfaction of our customers. Through this, we strive to meet your needs through knowledgeable service, dependability, honesty, and integrity.

Our Service Technicians

We have over 55 Whirlpool Certified technicians servicing multiple markets in Florida. To assure their success, each year they attend technical training classes where new products are introduced and also exemplary customer service is emphasized. We are a drug-free workplace and before joining our team, they must pass background checks, driver screening and a rigorous personal interview with our Service Manager, whom was a former technician and is a 30-year veteran of the appliance service industry. We also employ an in-house Training Manager who provides additional technical support to our technicians. We have individuals who pre-screen our calls and try to pre-identify the parts that will be needed to complete the service call all on the 1st trip to your home. Additionally, we stock the top moving parts on each of our service vehicles. Our technicians are only authorized to install Factory Certified Parts by the Manufacturer.

Our Support Staff

Our Support Staff work hand-in-hand with your technician. Our service representatives are knowledgeable and solution-oriented. They will work with you to coordinate a convenient service appointment and keep you advised of the progression of your repair. Our Parts Department also works hard to expedite all parts and receives deliveries twice a day from our Parts Distributor. They also receive direct shipments from the Manufacturer when needed. Our technicians are only authorized to install Factory Certified Parts by the Manufacturer.

Your Fields Appliance Service Experience

  • You will receive an automated call the day before you are scheduled, giving you a 3 hour window for your time of service.
  • Our technician will call you at least 30 minutes prior to arrival the day of your service.
  • Our technician will diagnose your appliance and if you are out of warranty give you a full written estimate for the repair.
  • If possible, our technician will arrive with pre-diagnosed parts in attempt to complete your repair on the 1st trip.
  • If parts are needed, our support team will expedite the delivery of any special-order parts.
  • Parts are couriered to every technician nightly for the next day's service calls.
  • Our technician will demonstrate the working appliance once the repair is made and answer any questions you may have.
  • Every technician takes special care to leave the repair area exactly as he found it.

Technology at its finest

Our Operating System was created by an individual that had many years of experience in the Appliance Service Industry. The entire Service process, from the time we are contacted to schedule service to the time the technician arrives at your home, is fully integrated. Your call is dispatched to us for final routing of the technician and allows us to locate all previous history on the specific appliance and communicate all current or past pertinent information to the technician.

Our technicians have laptop computers that enable them to access the manufacturers websites for the latest technical updates. They have full access to our part distributor's websites and are able to see the availability of needed parts. They are able to schedule any needed return visit based on the availability of the needed parts while in your home. Our office is able to see where a tech is on his route and if needed communicate his progression throughout the day to his customers. All information is uploaded back and forth every 10 minutes.

The diagnoses and the service the techs performs on your appliance is uploaded to our office every 10 minutes. This enables part orders to be placed and expedited throughout the day. The information on all dispatched service requests is also transmitted to the manufacturer multiple times a day.

Our technicians are equipped with GPS systems for ease in finding your home. Each technician has a smart phone which enables continual communication.
